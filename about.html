Our Story

About KhutbahCave

Making Friday khutbahs accessible to every Muslim, anywhere, anytime — for free.

KhutbahCave started as a simple idea: preserve and share the wisdom of Friday sermons so that anyone who missed a khutbah — or wants to revisit one — can do so easily.

We collect, format, and publish khutbahs from a variety of authors and traditions, all available for free on the web and through our mobile app.

What we offer

A growing library of khutbahs in English, carefully formatted for comfortable reading. You can search by topic, browse by author, and read on any device — no account required.

The mobile app

Our React Native app lets you take the library with you. Download khutbahs for offline reading and get notified when new content is added.

Contributing

If you'd like to contribute a khutbah or suggest a speaker, feel free to reach out. We're always looking to grow the library with quality content.
